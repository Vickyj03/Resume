Hobbies and Interests

I have many creative interests, a small sample of which can be viewed below

Animation

I have always been interested in the magic of animation, ever since I was a little girl. This has continued into my adult life and I am currently working towards a postrgraduate degree in 3D character animation. But it is the beauty of traditional 2D animation that has always captivated me and it is my dream to someday have the means to produce my own short animated masterpiece.

Comic book art

In addition to animation, comics and manga have been a fascination for me ever since I was introduced to 2000AD as a child. My absolute favourite comic book artists that have continued to inspire me to create the best art I can are Elf Quest's Wendy Pini and the truly talented Adam Warren.

Writing

When I was a stay at home mother with my second child, I took up creative writing and hired myself out briefly as a ghost writer of fiction. Although I thoroughly enjoyed the work, I wanted to create my own ideas under my own name and so now, I enjoy writing as a hobby.
